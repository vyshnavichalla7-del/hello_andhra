Hamsaladeevi Beach

overview

This pristine beach is at the confluence (Sagara Sangamam) where the Krishna River meets the Bay of Bengal, near Hamsaladeevi village. Known for peaceful, clean surroundings, holy dips during Magha Poornima, and proximity to Venugopala Swamy Temple.

Best time to Visit

  • Months: October to March
  • Time of Day: Early morning or late afternoon for best light and cool breezes.
  • Note: Avoid monsoons; the beach is quieter and ideal for solitude and photography during winter months.

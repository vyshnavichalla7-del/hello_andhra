kailasakona waterfalls

Overview of kailasakona Waterfalls

  • Location Located near Narayanavanam, in Tirupati District, Andhra Pradesh. Around 13 km from Puttur, 46 km from Tirupati, and 70 km from Chittoor. Easily accessible by road; falls lie close to the highway that connects Tirupati and Chennai.
  • Height: Approx. 40 feet tall, flowing year-round (perennial). Type: Single-drop waterfall with a clear, mineral-rich stream. Water collects into a natural pond below — visitors often bathe here. Known for tranquility, clean water, and proximity to Kailasanatha Swamy temple (dedicated to Lord Shiva and Goddess Parvati). Two smaller waterfalls (4 to 6 feet tall) also lie nearby on the approach path.
  • From the parking area, it’s a short 3–5 minute walk to the main waterfall. Path includes stone steps; terrain is manageable for most. Smaller falls are near the approach road, slightly off-trail, and may require light walking.

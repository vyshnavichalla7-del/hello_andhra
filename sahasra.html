    • The Sahasra Lingeswara Swamy Temple is a revered Hindu temple located in Ponnur, Guntur District, Andhra Pradesh. Dedicated to Lord Shiva, the temple is renowned for its unique feature: it houses 1,000 Shiva Lingas within a single sanctum, symbolizing the infinite nature of the deity. This distinctive aspect gives the temple its name, "Sahasra" meaning "thousand" in Sanskrit. The temple complex also includes a shrine to Sri Veeranjaneya Swamy (Lord Hanuman), highlighting the harmonious coexistence of different deities within the Hindu pantheon. The temple is situated approximately 30 km from Guntur on the Guntur-Bapatla-Chirala (GBC) Road, making it accessible for devotees and visitors alike.

kalyani revu waterfalls

Overview of kalyani revu Waterfalls

Location: Situated in the Koundinya Wildlife Sanctuary, approximately 10 km north of Palamaner in Chittoor District. Water Source: Fed by the Koundinya River, the waterfall is known for its deep canyon where water plunges steeply, creating a dramatic and scenic view. Alternate Name: Locally referred to as Kalyan Drive Falls.

Trek Details: The waterfall is accessible via a short walk of approximately 100 meters from the nearest road. Trail Condition: The path is relatively easy to navigate, but visitors should wear comfortable footwear and be cautious of uneven terrain. Parking: Parking facilities are available at the base, but during peak times, they may fill up quickly.

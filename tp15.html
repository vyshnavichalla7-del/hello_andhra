katiki waterfalls

Overview

katiki Waterfalls is a stunning natural waterfall located near Araku Valley in the Visakhapatnam district of Andhra Pradesh, about 7 km from the famous Borra Caves and around 90 km from Visakhapatnam city. Key Details: The waterfall is formed by the Gosthani River and cascades from a height of approximately 50-100 feet. Surrounded by lush green forests and rugged terrain, Katiki Waterfalls offers a serene and picturesque setting. To reach the falls, visitors take a short trek of about 20-30 minutes through narrow, sometimes slippery paths crossing streams and dense forest.

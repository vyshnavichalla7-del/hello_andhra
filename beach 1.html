Manginapudi Beach (Machilipatnam Beach / Bandar Beach)

overview

The most renowned beach in Krishna district, located about 11–15 km from Machilipatnam city. Known for its unique black soil (instead of regular sand), historic Dutch connections, and being a natural harbor. Major pilgrimage spot during Magha Poornima and famous for Datta Rameswaram and Dattashram Temple on the shore. Popular for safe bathing, horse riding, quad biking, and scenic sunsets.

Best time to Visit

  • Months: December to March (pleasant winter climate)
  • Time of Day: 6:00 AM – 9:00 AM (for sunrise, peaceful atmosphere) and 4:00 PM – 6:00 PM (for sunset; mild weather).
  • Note: Beach is open 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM, closed after sunset for safety; avoid visiting during monsoon (June–September) due to rough seas

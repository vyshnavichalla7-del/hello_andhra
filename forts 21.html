Penukonda Fort

History

Penukonda Fort served as the second capital of the Vijayanagara Empire after the fall of Hampi in 1565. It features impressive fortifications with bastions, gateways, and citadels. The fort includes structures like Gagan Mahal palace and several temples within its premises. It displays a mix of Hindu and Islamic architectural influences and played a strategic role in regional conflicts during Vijayanagara times

TU 142 aircraft museum

Overview

he TU-142 Aircraft Museum in Visakhapatnam is a unique aviation museum showcasing the Tupolev TU-142M, a Soviet-era maritime patrol and anti-submarine warfare aircraft used by the Indian Navy. The aircraft was in service for 29 years, accumulating 30,000 hours of accident-free flying before being decommissioned in 2017. Key Details: The museum is located near Ramakrishna Beach (RK Beach), opposite the INS Kurusura Submarine Museum on Beach Road, Visakhapatnam. The TU-142 aircraft is preserved and displayed in its entirety on a concrete platform, providing visitors a close-up view of the vast aircraft measuring about 53 meters in length, with a 50-meter wingspan. The museum features an exhibition hall showcasing various aircraft components such as engines, propellers, anti-submarine missiles, survival kits, sonobuoys, and data recorders, with detailed explanations.

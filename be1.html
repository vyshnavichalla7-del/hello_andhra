Kakinada Beach

History

Kakinada Beach, which lies along the long coastline of Andhra Pradesh, is perfect to unwind amongst its natural beauty and warm inviting sea waters. Known for its soft sands, gentle waves, and gorgeous sunsets, Kakinada Beach is not only a perfect spot for a refreshing seaside experience but also a place where you can connect with local life, watching fishermen at work, listening to the soothing sound of waves with boats coming in after their catch of the day, and breathing in the salty sea air.

Visiting timings

  • October to november
  • March to April
  • Winter,spring and shoulder seasons are the best to visit these beaches

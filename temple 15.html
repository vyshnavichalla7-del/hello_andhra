Sri Kotilinga Hari Hara Maha Kshetram

History

Situated on the banks of the Krishna River in Muktyala village, near Jaggayyapeta, Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh. The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva in the form of Sri Panchamukha Amrutha Lingeswara Swamy, a Shiva Lingam with five faces representing different forms of Shiva. Established in 1909, the temple is known for devotees performing Shiva Linga Prathishta (installation) ceremonies, with many Lingas already installed. It is said the temple will house one crore (ten million) Shiva Lingas eventually. The temple also contains shrines to Lord Shiva, Goddess Kamakshi Devi, and Sri Venkateswara Swamy. Nearby is an ancient temple with two Shiva Lingas and two Nandis, accessible via a descent of a few steps. The temple is highly revered and attracts devotees especially during Maha Shivaratri celebrations.

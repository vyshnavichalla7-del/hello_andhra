Iskon temple ananthapuram

History

The temple was inaugurated in February 2008, built by the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON) at a cost of about Rs 5 crore. It was established to propagate the teachings of Lord Krishna and the principles found in sacred texts like the Bhagavad Gita and Srimad Bhagavatam. The temple reflects traditional Vedic architectural styles and serves as a center for spiritual education, yoga, meditation, and cultural events aligned with Vaishnavism. The temple promotes devotional practices including kirtans (devotional singing) and celebrates festivals such as Janmashtami and Radhastami with great fervor, drawing many devotees.

Visiting Timings

  • The temple timings are generally:
  • 4:00 AM to 12:00 PM
  • 4:30 PM to 8:30 PM
  • It remains open daily, allowing visitors ample time for darshan and participation in rituals.

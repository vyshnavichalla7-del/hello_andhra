Panduranga Swamy Temple

History

The Panduranga Swamy Temple is located in Chilakalapudi, Machilipatnam, Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh. Built in 1929 by Sri Bhakta Narasimham, the temple holds spiritual significance among the local people. The deity and the sanctum are modeled closely after the famous Pandharpur temple in Maharashtra. An idol of Sri Abhayanjaneya Swami (an incarnation of Lord Hanuman) is placed in front of the main deity. Every year in the Kartika month, the temple celebrates a vibrant festival attracting many devotees.

Visiting Timings

  • Typical temple timings are morning and evening hours, but may vary during festivals.

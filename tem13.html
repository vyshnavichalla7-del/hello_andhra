Thalupulamma Thalli Temple

History

Talupulamma Thalli Temple is located in East Godavari district of Andhra Pradesh. This district is also known as God's Own District. The main deity of this temple is Talupulamma Thalli. This temple is frequently visited by the car and truck owners to seek blessings of Talupulamma Thalli Goddess. It is believed that goddess will save the vehicles from the accidents and also their business will flourish after seeking the blessings of hers. Now a days this temple has become a tourist attraction and people from all over Andhra Pradesh visits this temple. There is no dress code you need to wear any decent dress. Darshan are free for all however there are charges for special darshan.

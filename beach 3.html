Pedapatnam Beach (Nagayalanka Delta)

overview

Located towards the sea from Nagayalanka, it’s surrounded by mangroves and riverine delta landscapes. Known for being less crowded; a haven for bird-watchers and nature lovers.

Best time to Visit

  • Months: November to February (dry, birding season)
  • Time of Day: 7:00 AM – 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM – 6:00 PM
  • Note: Offbeat, so basic facilities may be limited; good for eco-tourism

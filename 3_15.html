sri sai baba temple

history

  • Location:
    • Situated atop a hill near the Seshachala Hills in Nagari, Chittoor District.
  • Legends:
    • The temple was established to honor Shirdi Sai Baba, a revered spiritual leader known for his teachings of love, compassion, and unity. The construction began with the Bhoomi Pooja on Guru Purnima Day in 2011, symbolizing the temple's dedication to spiritual growth and enlightenment.

    architecture

    The temple is constructed in a white marble structure, reflecting purity and simplicity. Features a 3D image of Sai Baba in the Dhyana Mandapam (meditation hall), where the eyes of Sai Baba appear to follow devotees, enhancing the spiritual experience. The main idol of Sai Baba is installed on the first floor, surrounded by serene surroundings conducive to meditation and prayer.

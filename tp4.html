VUDA park

Overview

VUDA Park, also known as Taraka Rama Park, is a major recreational and tourist attraction in Visakhapatnam (Vizag), Andhra Pradesh. It covers about 55 acres and is developed by the Visakhapatnam Urban Development Authority (VUDA). Located close to Ramakrishna Beach, the park offers lush gardens, panoramic sea views, and a variety of fun activities suitable for all age groups. Key Features and Attractions: Musical Water Fountain: A highlight with water dancing in sync to music and colorful lights, especially popular in the evenings. Roller Skating Rink: An international standard skating rink that attracts teenagers and young adults. Boating: Artificial lakes inside the park allow visitors to enjoy paddle boating and rowing.

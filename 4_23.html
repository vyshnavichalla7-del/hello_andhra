venkatagiri fort

overview

  • location
    • Venkatagiri Fort is located in Venkatagiri town, which is around: 65 km from Nellore 80 km from Tirupati 25 km from Gudur
  • history
    • The fort was originally built in the 17th century. It was ruled by the Velugoti dynasty, who were Padmanayaka Velama Rajas. The Venkatagiri rulers were feudatories of the Vijayanagara Empire and later under the Nawab of Arcot during Mughal rule. The Velugotis maintained semi-autonomous rule well into the British era, functioning as Zamindars under colonial administration.
  • Architecture & Layout
  • The fort has a classic South Indian royal palace design with Mughal and Rajput influences. Built primarily with stone and lime mortar, the structure features: Grand gateways Arched corridors Spacious courtyards Royal durbar halls Decorative balconies and pillars The central palace area once served as the residence of the Venkatagiri Rajas. There is a temple dedicated to Lord Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy inside the fort complex, reflecting the rulers' devotion.

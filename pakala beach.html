pakala beach

overview

Pakala is located on the shore of the Bay of Bengal, 5 km (3.1 mi) east of Singarayakonda and 3 km (1.9 mi) north of Ullapalem, with sea on the east. It is located at coordinates 15°16' N and 80°04' E at elevation starting at one foot near Pallepalem and about 25 ft (7.6 m) on the west side of village.

Visiting timings

  • Typically from morning 8 AM to Evening 6 AM

Mypadu beach

overview

  • location
    • Mypadu Beach is located on the Bay of Bengal coast, about 25 kilometers from Nellore city. It falls under Indukurpet Mandal of Tirupati district (previously Nellore).
  • more info
  • The beach is known for its serene coastline, golden sand, and clean waters. It is relatively less crowded, making it a peaceful spot for families, couples, and solo travelers. Sunrises and sunsets are particularly beautiful here due to the wide open view of the eastern horizon. The beach is well-maintained and supported by Andhra Pradesh Tourism (APTDC).

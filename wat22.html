Devarapalli Waterfalls

History

Surrounded by lush green forests and hills, this natural waterfall is a peaceful escape from busy city life. It is the perfect place for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and photographers. Devarapalli Waterfalls is near the Devarapalli village in East Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh

Visiting Timings

  • October to March is the best time..
  • Peak season is from November to January.
  • Waterfall is full and beautiful during these months
  • It is best to visit this waterfalls from 6:00 AM to 5:00 PM during day for safety.

Gooty Fort (Ravadurg)

History

Located near Gooty town, this hill fort is one of the oldest and largest forts in Andhra Pradesh. Built initially during the Chalukya period and later fortified by Vijayanagara rulers, it was also controlled by Qutb Shahi, Marathas, Mysore rulers, and the British. The fort incorporates 15 smaller forts with numerous bastions, gateways, and military structures. The fort offers panoramic views from its high vantage points and contains several temples and a Dargah inside

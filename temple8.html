sri appanna swamy temple- akiveedu

history

Sri Akiveedu Appanna Swamy Temple is a notable temple located in Akiveedu, West Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh. The temple is dedicated to Lord Appanna Swamy, a local deity revered by the community. Akiveedu is known for its spiritual heritage with several temples, including the Sri Venkateswara Swamy Temple and Sri Malleswara Swamy Temple, which attract devotees from surrounding regions. The Appanna Swamy temple plays a significant role in the local religious and cultural practices with regular festivals and rituals. The temple's serene environment and traditional architecture make it a cherished place of worship in the area

Tenneti park

Overview

Tenneti Park (also called VUDA Tenneti Beach Park) is a beautiful urban park located on Beach Road in Jodugullapalem, Visakhapatnam, overlooking the Bay of Bengal and the Kailasagiri Hills. It is one of the oldest and most scenic parks in the city. Key Details: The park was named after Tenneti Viswanadham, a notable freedom fighter and politician of Visakhapatnam, with a statue of him installed at the park entrance. Tenneti Park was severely damaged during Cyclone Hudhud in 2014 but later renovated with contributions from GITAM University alumni, restoring its greenery and improving facilities to international standards.

Madhavaraya temple history

History

The temple was built in 1354 AD during the reign of the Vijayanagara Empire under the Narasimha Saluva dynasty. It is a protected monument maintained by the Archaeological Survey of India (ASI). The temple features inscriptions and carvings from the Vijayanagara period and is known for its rich architectural style and religious significance.

Visiting Timings

  • Opening hours are typically from around 6:30 AM to 6:00 PM, though exact visiting hours may vary for special pujas and festivals.

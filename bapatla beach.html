Bapatla Beach

Overview

Suryalanka Beach, also known as Bapatla Beach, is a serene coastal destination located approximately 9 km from Bapatla town in Guntur District, Andhra Pradesh. Situated along the Bay of Bengal, the beach is renowned for its golden sands, clear waters, and tranquil atmosphere, making it an ideal spot for relaxation and leisure activities.

Visiting Timings

  • Open to Visitors 24 hours a day

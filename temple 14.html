Sri Bhoosameta Venkateswara Swamy Vari Devalayam

History

Known as the "Abhinava Tirumala," this temple is dedicated to Lord Sri Venkateswara along with Goddess Bhoosametha. It is located in Dokiparru village, Gudlavalleru Mandalam, Krishna District, Andhra Pradesh. The temple's architecture and rituals are inspired by the famous Tirumala Venkateswara temple, with similar sevas, pujas, and festivals. Constructed and supported by the family of PV Krishna Reddy, promoters of Megha Engineering Infrastructure Limited (MEIL), the temple hosts grand annual Brahmotsavam festivals. The temple features a golden dwaja sthambam (flagstaff), and various religious ceremonies such as Tulabharam, Rathotsavam, and Utsavas are performed with devotion. The temple has a strong community presence and is a major spiritual center close to the Tirumala temple tradition.

Visiting Timings

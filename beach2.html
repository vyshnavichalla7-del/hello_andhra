mollaparru beach

Overview

Mollaparru Beach is located near Perupalem in the West Godavari district of Andhra Pradesh, about 22 kilometers from Narasapur town. It is known for its picturesque and peaceful coastline, offering pleasant views to visitors. The beach is popular for its natural beauty and serves as a relaxing tourist spot alongside Perupalem Beach in the district. Efforts are underway to develop Mollaparru Beach for tourism alongside other coastal locations in the region.

Overview of Ubbalamadugu Waterfalls (Tada Falls)

  • Location: Situated in the Chittoor district of Andhra Pradesh, near the border with Tamil Nadu. The falls are approximately 35 km from Srikalahasti and 80 km from Chennai, making it a popular destination for visitors from both Andhra Pradesh and Tamil Nadu.
  • Height: The waterfall cascades from a height of about 100 meters, offering a stunning natural spectacle.
  • Surroundings: Nestled within the dense Siddulaiah Kona forest area, the falls are part of the Kambakam Forest region. The area is known for its rich biodiversity, including various flora and fauna.
  • Access: The trek to the falls is approximately 10 km from the base camp, with the last 4 km traversing rocky and uneven terrain. The journey offers scenic views and a chance to experience the forest's tranquility.

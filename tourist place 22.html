Anantapur Clock Tower

History

The Anantapur Clock Tower is a historic landmark built in memory of the martyrs of the Indian freedom movement. It was constructed in 1945 under the leadership of District Judge M. Ramachandra Naidu and inaugurated on August 15, 1947, the day India gained independence. The clock tower is located in the heart of Anantapur city.

Visiting Timings

  • The Clock Tower is an open public structure accessible throughout the day.
  • Visitors can view and take photographs at any time, with mornings and evenings being popular times for visiting.

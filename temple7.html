history

Sri Adikesava Swamy Temple, also known as Sri Adikesava Embermanar Swamy Temple, is located in Emburmanar Agraharam near Narsapur in West Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh. The temple is dedicated to Lord Adikesava, a form of Lord Vishnu, and holds special significance for Vaishnavites. Situated near the Vasista River (also called Vasista Godavari), the temple is often referred to as "Abhinava Bhoothapuri" of Andhra Pradesh, symbolizing a spiritual center akin to Sriperumbudur. The temple is renowned for its peaceful ambiance, devotional activities, and traditional architecture, attracting devotees who seek blessings from Lord Adikesava.

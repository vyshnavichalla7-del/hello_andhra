Perupalem beach

Overview

Perupalem Beach in West Godavari district is a tranquil and scenic beach along the Bay of Bengal, about 20 km from Narasapuram. Known for its clear blue waters and long sandy shore, it is ideal for evening strolls, swimming, and family outings. The beach is also significant for religious rituals, especially during the Kartikam month. It offers a peaceful environment with nearby temples and attracts visitors seeking both relaxation and cultural experiences. Though still developing, it is becoming a popular tourist spot with local food and nearby accommodations available in Narasapuram.

Popular Destinations

srikakulam

Srikakulam is a coastal town in Andhra Pradesh, known for its ancient temples and scenic beaches. It has a rich history and is located near the Bay of Bengal, making it both culturally and naturally beautiful..

vizayanagaram

Vizianagaram is a historic town in Andhra Pradesh, known for its royal heritage and cultural significance. It was once a princely state and is famous for its forts, palaces, and classical music tradition. .

visakhapatnam

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is a major coastal city in Andhra Pradesh. It is known for its beautiful beaches, industrial growth, and naval base. Vizag blends natural beauty with urban development, making it a key tourist and economic hub..

Learn More

krishna

Krishna is a district in Andhra Pradesh, known for its fertile lands, thanks to the Krishna River. It has a rich cultural and historical background, with Vijayawada as its major city. The region is famous for agriculture, temples, and classical arts.

Learn More

east godvari

East Godavari is a lush and fertile district in Andhra Pradesh, known for its rich agriculture, scenic landscapes, and the Godavari River. It is famous for paddy fields, coconut groves, and cultural heritage, with towns like Rajahmundry and Kakinada playing key roles in its economy and tradition.

Learn More

west godavari

West Godavari is a fertile and culturally rich district in Andhra Pradesh, known for its agriculture, especially paddy and sugarcane. The Godavari River supports its greenery, and towns like Eluru are key centers. The district is also known for traditional arts, temples, and scenic beauty. .

Learn More

guntur

Guntur is a major district in Andhra Pradesh, known for its agriculture, especially chili production. It has a strong historical and cultural background, with the city of Guntur serving as a key educational and commercial hub. The region also plays an important role in politics and trade.

prakasam

.Prakasam is a district in Andhra Pradesh known for its rich mineral resources, especially granite and limestone. It has a mix of agriculture and industry, with Ongole as its main city, famous for the Ongole breed of cattle. The district also has a long coastline and cultural heritage.

nellore

Nellore is a coastal district in Andhra Pradesh, known for agriculture, aquaculture, and rich cultural heritage. It is famous for paddy fields, shrimp farming, and temples. Nellore city is the district headquarters and plays a key role in trade and education. .

kurnool

Kurnool is a historic city and district in Andhra Pradesh, known as the “Gateway to Rayalaseema.” It has a rich cultural heritage, famous for ancient forts like Kurnool Fort and religious sites. The region is also important for agriculture and has a growing industrial base.

chittoor

Chittoor is a district in Andhra Pradesh known for its rich cultural heritage and agricultural produce, especially mangoes and sugarcane. It is home to the famous Tirupati temple, one of the most visited pilgrimage sites in India. The district combines natural beauty with spiritual significance. .

kadapa

Kadapa, also known as YSR district, is located in Andhra Pradesh and is known for its mineral wealth, especially limestone and barytes. It has a rich history, beautiful temples, and is an important agricultural and industrial center in the region. .

Learn More

anantapuram

Anantapuram (Anantapur) is a district in Andhra Pradesh known for its dry climate and historic significance. It is famous for the Lepakshi temple and its silk industry. Despite being arid, the region has a rich cultural heritage and is an important center for agriculture and education.

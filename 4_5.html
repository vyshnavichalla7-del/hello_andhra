sri malesthneswari swamy temple

History

  • location

    • : Mulapeta area, about 2 km west of the Nellore bus station.

  • legend

    • It is said that a cowherd discovered milk flowing mysteriously from a cow onto a rock, which later oozed blood, leading to the temple's foundation. The temple houses several important shrines including: Sri Moolastaneswara Swamy (main deity) Sri Bhuvaneshwari Devi (Goddess) Sri Vinayaka Swamy (Ganesh) Sri Subrahmanyaswamy (Karthikeya) Sri Kala Bhairava Swamy Sri Virabhadra Swamy Sri Sahasra Lingeswara Swamy Sri Saraswati Ammavaru Sri Durga Devi Ammavaru Sri Dakshina Murthy Swamy Navagrahas (Nine planetary deities) Sri Nataraja Swamy Sri Satyabhama Sri Venugopala Swamy Sri Surya Narayana Swamy

  • architecture

    • Features classic Kakatiya-style architecture with intricate carvings and a prominent Nandi Mandapam (hall dedicated to Lord Shiva’s bull) Believed to have been established in the 11th century during the Kakatiya dynasty’s reign.

History

  • loacation

    • Vengamamba Temple at Narrawada,Duttalur Mandal

  • legend

    • Sri Vengamamba is worshipped here as a powerful Grama Devata (village goddess), and many believe she grants wishes, particularly related to health, marriage, business, and childbearing. Devotees follow a tradition known as "Vengamma Dabbu", where they take a small loan in the name of the goddess during the festival, with the belief that they must return it the next year after gaining prosperity. The temple is believed to be more than 300 years old, with stories passed down through generations of miracles and divine interventions.

    .

  • architecture

    • Traditional South Indian village-style temple. It features a simple gopuram (tower), an open courtyard, and a main sanctum where the idol of Goddess Vengamamba is housed. The temple premises also include small shrines for other deities, and a large area for community gatherings during festivals.

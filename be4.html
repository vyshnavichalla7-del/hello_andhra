Odalrevu Beach

History

Great beach for a pleasant stroll and to enjoy some peace along the waves as there is very less crowd here Odalarevu Beach is a long fine sandy coastline. Wild, with no amenities, relatively uncrowded during the season. This beach is clean. Located just 2.2 km away from the centre of Arukettapalem

Visiting timings

  • October to november
  • March to April
  • Winter,spring and shoulder seasons are the best to visit these beaches

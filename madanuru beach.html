madanur beach

overview

Madanur Beach is situated in the Bay of Bengal (part of Indian ocean) and ranks the 76th among 115 beaches of the Andhra Pradesh region. The distance to its main city - Ongole is 18 km. The nearest settlement is Madanuru, the centre of which is 1.2 km away.

Visiting timings

  • Typically from morning 8 AM to Evening 6 AM

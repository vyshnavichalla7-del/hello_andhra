Singarakonda Temple

History

The Singarakonda Temple refers to a sacred complex on Singarakonda (Singarayakonda) hill in the Prakasam district of Andhra Pradesh, dedicated to Sri Lakshminarasimha Swamy and Sri Prasannanjaneya Swamy. Historically, a temple to Lord Narasimha was built on the hill during the 14th century, and the site later became known as Singarakonda.

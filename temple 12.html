Sri Kondalamma Ammavari Temple

History

This temple is dedicated to Sri Kondalamma Ammavari, a revered local goddess believed to grant blessings on childbirth, marriages, and other family welfare issues. The temple's origin dates back approximately four decades when a stone resembling the goddess was discovered during road construction near Vemavaram village in Krishna district. The stone was kept aside and later worshipped, leading to the establishment of the temple. It is believed that the goddess grants devotees' wishes, especially for children and marital happiness, attracting large crowds of worshippers. Devotees traditionally offer their thanks and prayers after their wishes are fulfilled, establishing a strong community faith around this temple.

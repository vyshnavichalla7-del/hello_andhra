History

  • location

    • Sri Venugopala Swamy Temple located in Mulapeta, near Nellore, Andhra Pradesh

  • legend

    • .The Sri Venugopala Swamy Temple in Mulapeta is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, specifically in his form as Venugopala, the divine flutist. The temple is known for its rich history, architectural beauty, and spiritual significance in the region.

    .

  • architecture

    • .Main Shrine (Garbhagriha): Houses the idol of Lord Venugopala Swamy, depicted playing his flute. Subshrines: Dedicated to various deities, enhancing the temple's spiritual ambiance. Mandapam: A pillared hall used for congregational worship and festivals. Sacred Pool (Kollugunta): Located at the western corner of the temple, believed to have healing properties for mental and physical ailments.

Bhairavakona Waterfalls

History

Bhairavakona Temple and Waterfalls is an ancient rock-cut cave temple located in Ambavaram, Prakasam district, Andhra Pradesh. Nestled within the dense Nallamala Hills, this sacred site is known for its spiritual significance, stunning waterfalls, and mythological history

Visiting Timings

  • October to March is the best time..
  • Peak season is from November to January.
  • Waterfall is full and beautiful during these months
  • It is best to visit this waterfalls from 6:00 AM to 5:00 PM during day for safety, as the area is surrounded by dense forest.

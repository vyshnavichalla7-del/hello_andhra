swarnala cheruvu

overview

  • location
    • Swarnala Cheruvu, also known as the Golden Lake, is a well-known water body located in Nellore city, Andhra Pradesh. It is situated approximately 3 kilometers from Nellore Railway Station, making it easily accessible.
  • Attractions
    • The lake provides a peaceful and serene environment, perfect for morning and evening walks. On its banks is the famous Bara Shaheed Dargah, where an annual festival called Rottela Panduga (Roti Festival) is celebrated. This festival symbolizes community unity and harmony through the exchange of flatbreads. The lake area also hosts the Ganesh Ghat, which becomes lively during festivals like Ganesh Chaturthi and Maha Karthika Deepotsavam, with processions and idol immersions.

It has facilities like a goshala (cow shelter), guest house, gift shop, and runs charitable food programs.

Visiting Timings

  • Morning (4:30 AM – 9:30 AM): Mangala Aarthi, Tulasi Aarthi, Japa meditation, darshan, and spiritual classes.
  • Afternoon (12:00 PM – 1:00 PM): Bhoga Aarthi followed by rest (temple closed for darshan between 1 PM and 4:30 PM).
  • Evening (4:30 PM – 9:00 PM): Evening darshan, aartis, and spiritual classes.
  • Special Sunday Feast: Served from 9 PM to 10 PM for devotees.

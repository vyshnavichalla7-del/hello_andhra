History

Valandarevu is a popular backwater point located near Narasapuram in the West Godavari district of Andhra Pradesh. It lies at the confluence of the Godavari River and the Bay of Bengal, where tranquil backwaters and scenic riverine surroundings create a peaceful environment. This area is known for its cluster of islands, coconut orchards, and rich birdlife, making it an attractive spot for nature lovers and tourists seeking quiet boat rides and island hopping experiences.

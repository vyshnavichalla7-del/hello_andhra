Overview

Visakha Museum, officially known as the Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation Museum, is a prominent museum located on Beach Road near Ramakrishna Beach in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. It preserves and showcases the rich history, culture, and heritage of the Kalinga Andhra region. Key Details: The museum was inaugurated on October 8, 1991, in a renovated Dutch bungalow setting. It has two main sections: Maritime Museum: Founded in collaboration with the Eastern Naval Command, it exhibits naval artifacts, ship models, and maritime history related to Visakhapatnamâ€™s port and Navy. Heritage Museum: Displays archaeological artifacts, sculptures, ancient coins, paintings, old weapons, textiles, musical instruments, and historical relics from the region.

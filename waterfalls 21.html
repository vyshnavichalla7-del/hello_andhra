Konauppalapadu Kona Falls

significance

The falls are part of the natural landscape in the Anantapur region and are appreciated for their tranquility and scenic beauty. It attracts nature lovers and visitors looking for a quiet spot away from crowded tourist centers. The water reportedly has healing properties, which adds to the mystique and appeal of the spot among locals and visitors. It is a favored picnic and trekking destination, especially during the monsoon when the water flow is strong.

Visiting Timings

  • The falls are generally open for visitors from morning to evening.
  • Typical visiting hours are approximately from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
  • Best time to visit is during the monsoon and post-monsoon season (October to January) when the waterfall is full and the surrounding hills are lush green.

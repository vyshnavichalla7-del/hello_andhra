Tyda nature camp

Overview

Tyda Nature Camp, also known as Jungle Bells, is an eco-tourism destination located about 40 km from Araku Valley and around 70 km from Visakhapatnam in the Eastern Ghats. It is a small tribal village nestled amidst dense forests, offering visitors a serene natural environment away from city life. Key Features: The camp provides facilities for rock climbing, trekking, bird watching, and target shooting with bow and arrows. Visitors can learn about the jungle ecosystem, including identifying bird calls and animal tracks, with the help of naturalists or guides. Accommodation options include log huts and tents that allow guests to experience tribal life and nature intimately. The camp also organizes tribal dance performances and bonfires, enhancing cultural engagement.

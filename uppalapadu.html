uppalapadu bird sanctuary

history

The Uppalapadu Bird Sanctuary is generally open from 7:30 AM to 6:00 PM daily, though some sources suggest a slightly different timing of 6:00 AM to 6:00 PM. It's a good idea to arrive early in the morning or late in the afternoon for the best bird-watching experience, particularly during the migratory season from October to March.

Visiting Timings

  • Open: 7:30 AM (or 6:00 AM)
  • Close: 6:00 PM

Best to Visit

  • Best Time to Visit: The migratory season, from October to March, is ideal for seeing various bird species.
  • Best Time of Day: Visit in the early morning or late afternoon for the best chances to spot birds.

Tripuranthakam Bala thripura devi Temple

History

The history of Tripuranthakam and its Bala Tripura Sundari Devi Temple is linked to the legend of Lord Shiva destroying the three cities of the demons, earning him the name Tripuranthakeswara, and the town being named Tripuranthakam. The temple complex, built by the Kakatiya dynasty in the 11th to 16th centuries, houses both the Sri Bala Tripurantakeswara Swamy temple and the Sri Bala Tripurasundari Devi temple. The temple is also known for a secret underground passage connecting it to Srisailam.

Yanam Beach

History

Yanam River Beach offers a peaceful escape along the banks of the Godavari River. This serene stretch of sandy shoreline has become increasingly popular with both locals and tourists seeking respite from the bustling city life. I’ve spent countless evenings here watching the sun paint the sky in brilliant oranges and pinks while fishing boats drift lazily across the horizon. The beach’s unique charm lies in its blend of river and coastal vibes – something you don’t find at your typical oceanfront destinations

Visiting timings

  • October to november
  • March to April
  • Winter,spring and shoulder seasons are the best to visit these beaches

Sri Sai Giri Temple

History

The temple was established by a group of devoted followers who formed the Sri Sai Baba Seva Samithi. They were inspired by Sai Baba's teachings and decided to build a temple in his honor. The foundation stone was laid on November 22, 2001, marking the beginning of the temple's construction .The temple is constructed in a modern architectural style, featuring a life-sized statue of Sai Baba. The main sanctum houses a marble idol of Sai Baba, surrounded by intricately designed walls adorned with images depicting various aspects of his life and teachings . Sri Sai Giri Temple serves as a spiritual center for devotees, offering a serene environment for meditation and prayer. It stands as a testament to the enduring legacy of Sai Baba's philosophy, attracting visitors seeking peace and spiritual solace.

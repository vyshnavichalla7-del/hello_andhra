penchalakona waterfalls

overview

  • location
    • Penchalakona Waterfalls is located near Penchalakona village in Rapur Mandal, Nellore district, Andhra Pradesh. It is about 70 kilometers from Nellore city. To reach the waterfalls, visitors usually start from the nearby Penusila Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple and then trek about 2 kilometers through a forested path.
  • Attractions
    • The waterfall is the origin of the Kandaleru River, which is important both ecologically and culturally in the region. The area around the waterfall is rich in dense forests and wildlife, including various birds, monkeys, and other animals.

eluru budha park

overview

Eluru Buddha Park, located in the heart of Eluru city in Andhra Pradesh, is a serene park dedicated to Lord Buddha. It features a majestic 74-feet tall Buddha statue situated in the middle of the ancient pond called Gaja Vallivari Cheruvu. The statue's pedestal is adorned with famous Amaravathi sculptures, and the park includes a painting gallery to spread the teachings of Buddha. The park symbolizes peace, harmony, and Eluru's rich Buddhist heritage dating back to the 2nd century BC during the Mauryan Empire. It serves as a popular cultural and tourist destination, open daily from early morning to evening, and has facilities to enhance visitor experience.

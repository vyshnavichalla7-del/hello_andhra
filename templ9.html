Prudulgiri Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple

History

The "Prudhulagiri Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple" is likely referring to the Sri Vedagiri Lakshimi Narasimha Swamy Temple at Narasimha Konda in the Nellore District of Andhra Pradesh. This ancient temple, located near Simhapuri (Nellore), is a sacred site dedicated to Lord Narasimha, an avatar of Vishnu, and is mentioned in the Brahmanda Purana. It is characterized by a prominent, 350-foot-high hillock that forms the sacred kshetra, or holy ground.

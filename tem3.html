Sri vasavi Kanyaka parameswari temple

History

The Sri Vasavi Kanyaka Parameshwari Temple in Pamuru, Andhra Pradesh, is a prominent Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Vasavi Kanyaka Parameswari, the tutelary deity of the Arya Vysya community. While there isn't detailed information readily available about the Pamuru temple itself, the deity is widely celebrated, especially during the Dasara (Navaratri) festivals, and similar temples exist in other cities like Proddatur, which is known for its grand celebrations and impressive architecture

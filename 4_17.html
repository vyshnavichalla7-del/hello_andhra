penna barrage

overview

  • location

    • The Penna Barrage is located near Pothireddypalem, about 5 km from Nellore city. It is built across the Penna River to manage water flow, support irrigation, and provide road connectivity.

  • more info

    • Construction began around 2008 and was completed by 2022. The barrage has 43 spillway gates and 8 scour vents to control river water and prevent sediment buildup. It has a storage capacity of approximately 0.45 TMC (thousand million cubic feet) of water. Designed to irrigate nearly 99,000 acres of farmland in the Penna delta region. Includes a 7.5-meter wide road on top, connecting nearby mandals and easing transportation.

  • Importance
    • The barrage is vital for ensuring a steady water supply for agriculture in the area, stabilizing irrigation, and improving crop yields. It also improves road connectivity between local towns, benefiting the regionâ€™s economy.
  • Challenges
    • The project faced delays due to funding and logistical issues but was successfully completed after many years

Ethipothala Waterfalls

Overview

Ethipothala Falls is a captivating 70-foot (21-meter) waterfall situated in the Palnadu district of Andhra Pradesh, near the Nagarjuna Sagar Dam. Formed by the confluence of three streams—Chandravanka Vagu, Nakkala Vagu, and Tummala Vagu—the waterfall cascades into a serene pool, eventually merging with the Krishna River. The name "Ethipothala" translates to "lift and pour," reflecting the majestic descent of the water. The area is renowned for its lush greenery, making it a popular spot for nature enthusiasts and photographers.

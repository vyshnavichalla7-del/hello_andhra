Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple

History

The Sri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple, also known as the Malakonda Temple or Sri Malyadri Lakshmi Narasimha Swamy Temple, is a famous temple dedicated to Lord Lakshmi Narasimha located on the Malakonda hill in Valetivari Palem, Prakasam District, Andhra Pradesh.

Visiting Timings

  • Open: 5:30 AM
  • Close: 5:30 PM
  • Only Open on Saturdays for Darshan

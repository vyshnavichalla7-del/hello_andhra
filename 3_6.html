kaigal waterfalls

Overview of kaigal Waterfalls

Located in Chittoor district, Andhra Pradesh, about 6 km from Baireddipalle and roughly 80 km from Chittoor town. The waterfall is around 40 feet high, formed by the Kaigal stream flowing through the Koundinya Wildlife Sanctuary. The local name "Dumukurallu" means “jumping stones,” inspired by the sound of water hitting the rocks.

Moderate trekking through the forest to reach the falls. Swimming is possible in natural pools at the base. Great spot for photography and enjoying nature. The area is part of Koundinya Wildlife Sanctuary, so you may spot various birds and animals

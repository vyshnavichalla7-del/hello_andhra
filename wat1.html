Dharapalli Waterfalls

History

The trekking distance to Dharapalli Waterfalls is around 1 to 1.5 kilometers from the parking area. The trail goes through a forest with some rocky and uneven paths. It is a moderate trek, and wearing good trekking shoes is recommended. Located near Peddipalem in Prathipadu Mandal. 35 km from Pithapuram, 20 km from Prathipadu. The road is rough, and the last stretch needs a short trek. Jeeps can be best to reach the falls.

Visiting Timings

  • October to March is the best time..
  • Peak season is from November to January.
  • Waterfall is full and beautiful during these months
  • It is best to visit this waterfalls from 6:00 AM to 5:00 PM during day for safety, as the area is surrounded by dense forest.

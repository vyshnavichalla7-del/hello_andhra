Sunnapugedda waterfalls

OverView

Sunnapugeda Waterfalls, nestled in the lush forests of Seethampeta in Srikakulam district, Andhra Pradesh, is a serene natural retreat. Located approximately 15.5 km from NTR Adventure Park, this hidden gem offers a tranquil escape amidst dense greenery and cascading waters. Scenic Trekking: Embark on a forest trail leading to the waterfall, offering opportunities for birdwatching and immersion in nature. Photography: Capture the mesmerizing views of the waterfall and surrounding landscapes. Cultural Exploration: Engage with the local tribal communities to learn about their traditions and way of life.

Visiting Time to visit

  • October to March

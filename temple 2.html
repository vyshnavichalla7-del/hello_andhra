Sri Subramanyeshwara Swamy Temple, Mopidevi

History

Sri Subramanyeshwara Swamy Temple at Mopidevi is a revered Hindu temple dedicated to Lord Subramanya (Kartikeya). The temple is believed to be centuries old and holds great religious significance in the Krishna district of Andhra Pradesh. It is known for its beautiful Dravidian architecture and serene surroundings. Devotees from across the region visit the temple to seek blessings, especially during festivals like Skanda Shashti.

Visiting Timings

  • Morning: 6:00 AM to 12:00 PM
  • Evening: 4:00 PM to 8:00 PM
  • Open all days of the week

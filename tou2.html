Cumbum hills

History

The "Cumbum Hills" refers to the Nallamala Hills that surround the Cumbum Lake, a large, ancient man-made irrigation tank in the Prakasam district of Andhra Pradesh, India. This area features the Cumbum tank, an impressive feat of 15th-century Vijayanagara engineering, which draws visitors interested in history, nature, and photography.

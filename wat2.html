Rampa Waterfalls

History

Nestled in the lush, green hills of the Eastern Ghats near Rampachodavaram village, the Rampa Waterfalls is one of the most beautiful and lesser-known attractions in the East Godavari district of Andhra Pradesh. Surrounded by dense forest, this waterfall is a perfect spot for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and anyone looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.The Rampa Waterfalls Rampachodavaram is one of the highest in Andhra Pradesh, cascading from a height of 50 feet. Visiting this waterfall is not only about witnessing nature’s beauty but also experiencing a thrilling jeep ride through the dense jungle to reach the falls. If you’re seeking an adventure mixed with tranquility, Rampa Waterfalls won’t disappoint.

Visiting Timings

  • October to March is the best time..
  • Peak season is from November to January.
  • Waterfall is full and beautiful during these months
  • It is best to visit this waterfalls from 6:00 AM to 5:00 PM during day for safety, as the area is surrounded by dense forest.

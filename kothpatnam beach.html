Kothapatnam Beach

History

Kothapatnam Beach is one amongst the top attractions in Ongole. Located 18 kilometres (11 mi) from the city, the beach is a great source of entertainment for local people and tourists. The clear blue waters of the sea, the long stretches of sand and the gentle breeze mesmerize you. Boating is available here. But for the Karthika Purnima festival, when locals throng in thousands, the beach is less frequented.

Visiting timings

  • Typically from morning 8 AM to Evening 6 AM

Addhariperta Beach

History

This calm and serene beach offers a picturesque view of the golden sand and the stretch of hills. Bordered by hills on three sides and the Bay of Bengal on the other, this can be your next vacation stop. In contrast to Rishikonda, this is less crowded and yet to be commercialised

Visiting timings

  • October to november
  • March to April
  • Winter,spring and shoulder seasons are the best to visit these beaches

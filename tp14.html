Lambasingi

Overview

Lambasingi is a picturesque small village located in the Eastern Ghats of Chintapalli Mandal, Alluri Sitharama Raju district, Andhra Pradesh, about 105 km from Visakhapatnam (Vizag). Known as the "Kashmir of Andhra Pradesh," it is famous for its cool climate and is the only place in South India that experiences snowfall during winter months, from December to February. Key Features: Situated at an altitude of around 1,000 meters (3,280 feet) above sea level. The village is surrounded by lush green landscapes, dense moist deciduous forests, and plantations of coffee, pepper, pineapples, and strawberries. Lambasingi experiences chilling temperatures and often low fog levels in the winter, with occasional snowfall that attracts tourists for its rare phenomenon in southern India.

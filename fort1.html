Kanigiri Fort

History

Kanigiri Fort is a historic monument and tourist attraction located on a hill in the town of Kanigiri, which is in the Prakasam district of Andhra Pradesh, India. It is the site of a former capital of the kingdom of Katama Raju, and historical features like ancient wells are found within its vicinity. The fort is open 24 hours, is free to visit, and offers enjoyable views from the hilltop, though climbing it can be challenging

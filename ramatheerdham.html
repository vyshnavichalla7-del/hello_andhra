Ramatheerdham

History

Ramatheerdham, located near Vizianagaram in Nellimarla Mandal, Andhra Pradesh, is an ancient and sacred pilgrimage site. It is believed that Lord Rama, Sita, and Lakshmana visited this place during their exile (Vanavasa), making it highly revered by devotees. The site includes the Sri Ramachandra Swamy Temple, with silver-plated idols of Lord Rama and his family. Additionally, the region has historical remnants of Jain and Buddhist heritage, such as caves and sculptures, indicating that it was an important center of spiritual activity in ancient times. Ramatheerdham is considered a powerful spiritual place, attracting devotees seeking blessings for peace, prosperity, and knowledge.

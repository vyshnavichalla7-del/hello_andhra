Overview of horsely hills

Horsley Hills – The “Ooty of Andhra Pradesh

  • Location Horsley Hills is a scenic hill station in Andhra Pradesh, located near Madanapalle in Annamayya (formerly Chittoor) district. The altitude is approximately 1,265 meters above sea level, offering a cool climate and forested terrain.
  • Viewpoints: Several cliff-edge viewpoints offer stunning views of the surrounding hills and valleys, especially at sunrise and sunset. Gali Bandalu (Wind Rocks): Famous for strong gusts of wind and panoramic landscapes. Environmental Park: A small nature park with walking paths and greenery. Mini Zoo & Deer Park: Great for kids and nature lovers — peacocks, deer, and other small animals can be seen. Kalyani Eucalyptus Tree: A giant tree over 100 years old — a local landmark. Mallamma Temple: A peaceful temple with a local legend attached to it. Trekking & Nature Walks: Short and easy trails for walking through the forest.

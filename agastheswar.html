Sri Agastheswar Swamy Temple

History

Believed to have been established by the sage Agastya during the Treta Yuga, the temple's origins are steeped in legend. The current structure dates back to the 12th century, as evidenced by inscriptions found within the temple premises. These inscriptions provide insights into the temple's rich heritage and its role in the religious landscape of the time. Apart from its religious importance, the Sri Agasteswara Swamy Temple is also a testament to the architectural and cultural heritage of Guntur. Its historical inscriptions and traditional rituals offer a glimpse into the ancient practices and devotion that have been preserved through generations.

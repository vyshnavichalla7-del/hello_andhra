sri vasavi kanyaka parameswari temple

history

Sri Vasavi Kanyaka Parameswari Temple is a prominent temple located in Penugonda, West Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh. It is dedicated to Goddess Vasavi Kanyaka Parameswari, who is revered as the Kuladevata (family deity) by the Arya Vaishya community and is seen as a symbol of peace, dharma, and sacrifice. The temple is often called the "Kasi of Vysyas" due to its spiritual significance for this community. Architecturally, the temple features a large garbha griha, tall Raja Gopuram, and intricate sculptures, following traditional vastu shastra. Penugonda, situated on the banks of the Gautami River, is considered a sacred site with a rich spiritual history linked to several saints and deities. The temple hosts important festivals like Vasavi Matha Athmarpana Day and Devi Navaratrulu and welcomes devotees with special rituals and daily worship

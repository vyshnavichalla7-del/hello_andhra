IDTA Mettaguda Waterfalls

History & Significance

Tribal Heritage: The area around Mettuguda is predominantly inhabited by tribal communities, including the Savara tribe. Their rich cultural heritage and traditions add a unique dimension to the region. Adventure Tourism Development: The Integrated Tribal Development Agency (ITDA) has been instrumental in promoting eco-tourism in Seethampeta. Efforts include developing infrastructure and organizing activities to attract visitors while preserving the region's ecological balance. Waterfall Trekking: Visitors can embark on a trek through dense forests to reach the waterfall. The journey offers a chance to experience the region's rich biodiversity and scenic landscapes. NTR Adventure Park: Located approximately 4 km from Mettuguda, this park offers various adventure activities, including paramotoring, ATV rides, and wall climbing. It serves as a hub for adventure tourism in the region. Cultural Experiences: Engaging with the local tribal communities provides insights into their traditional lifestyles, crafts, and customs.

Visiting Months

    The ideal months are from October to March

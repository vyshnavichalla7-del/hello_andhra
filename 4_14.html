sri Nava Bala Durga temple

History

  • location

    • Situated on Pottepalem Main Road, close to Nellore city center. Around 2 to 3 kilometers from Nellore Railway Station. Easily accessible by auto or bus within city limits.

  • legend

    • The temple is dedicated to Sri Nava Bala Durga Devi, a powerful form of Goddess Durga. "Nava Bala" refers to the Nine Powers or Strengths of the Goddess, believed to represent her as a force that protects devotees through all stages of life. Many devotees believe that worshipping here brings: Protection from evil forces Success in exams or jobs Relief from family disputes Marriage blessings Health and fertility benefits

    .

  • architecture

    • .The temple features a traditional South Indian design with: A simple yet beautifully decorated Gopuram (entrance tower) Garbhagriha (sanctum) housing the idol of Goddess Nava Bala Durga Devi, adorned with sarees, flowers, kumkum, and turmeric Spacious mandapam (pillared hall) for devotees to sit and pray Small shrines for Ganapati, Navagrahas, and other deities within the complex The temple premises are clean and maintained with devotion by locals and temple staff.

Visiting Timings

  • Morning: Opens by 6:30 AM Closes around 10:30 to 11:00 AM on regular days
  • On Fridays and Sundays, it remains open till 12:30 PM Evening hours may vary, especially during festivals or special pujas

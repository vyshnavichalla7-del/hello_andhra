Sri Abaya Anjaneya swamy Temple

History

The Sri Abhaya Anjaneya Swamy Temple in Prakasam District is likely referring to the recently famous temple in Khajipuram village, near Cumbum. This temple is known for its 54-foot-tall sitting Anjaneya idol, which is considered the largest sitting statue of Lord Hanuman in Andhra Pradesh. The temple is a significant religious site in the area, attracting devotees from far and wide to witness the massive monolithic stone idol of Lord Anjaneya.

Sri Anjaneya swamy and Nagendra Swamy Temple

History

This ancient temple, potentially built around the 7th century, is located in Prakasam district. According to local folklore, the temple's importance stems from the legend that Goddess Seetha worshipped a Shiva Lingam here to secure the betterment of Sri Rama. The temple also features a shrine to a West-facing Anjaneya Swamy (Baktha Anjaneya Swamy) and a Garuda Alwar sub-shrine.

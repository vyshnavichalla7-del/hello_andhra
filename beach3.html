vemuladevi beach

overview

Vemuladevi Beach is a serene and less crowded beach located in the village of Vemuladevi, Narasapur Mandal, West Godavari district, Andhra Pradesh. It is about 45 km from Bhimavaram and 15 km from Palakollu. The beach offers peaceful surroundings with golden sands, clear waters, and scenic views of coconut plantations and local fishing activities. Nearby, there is the famous Vemuladevi Venkateswara Swamy Temple, adding a spiritual dimension to the visit. The beach is ideal for relaxing evenings, nature walks, and experiencing rural coastal life. It remains a hidden gem for tourists seeking calmness and natural beauty away from crowded spots.

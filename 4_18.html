kanadleru dam

overview

  • location
    • The Kandaleru Dam is a significant irrigation project located on the Kandaleru River in Rapur Mandal, Nellore district, Andhra Pradesh. This earth dam is approximately 12 kilometers long and plays a crucial role in water supply and flood control for the region.
  • more info

    • Storage Capacity: The Kandaleru Reservoir has a gross storage capacity of 72 billion cubic feet. Design: The dam includes a head regulator, spillway, and a sundial, with the lead engineer being Shri Gudimella Raghupathi. Purpose: It is part of the Telugu Ganga project, which supplies drinking water to Chennai city from the Srisailam Reservoir on the Krishna River.

Visiting Timings

    Best Time to Visit: The ideal time to visit is during the cooler months, from October to March, to enjoy the scenic views and pleasant weather.
